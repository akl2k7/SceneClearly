About Us

SCENE CLEARLY LLC is a window cleaning company specializing in making your day a little brighter.

We take care of window cleaning services so that you don't have to worry about it. We perform residential and commercial work. We are able to work either on a per time basis or by setting up a schedule so your windows don't become unbearable.

History

Frank Cordova Jr. began cleaning windows for a janitorial service in 1990. Later on he went to work for a busy window cleaning company in the Grand Junction area. Taking care of regular services for one of our local Hospitals and many other businesses and houses in our area. Later on Frank Sr. became Frank Jr's partner in 2000 and completely retired at the end of December 2009. We are a smaller business we have had a maximum of three other employees cleaning along with us. Our motto is that if you are trustworthy and reliable and do a good, clean job, it would help in obtaining and keeping our work. It really has proven true.

Prices

Due to the fact that all houses and buildings are different, we have to see the house or the building to give a more precise price. Many times your name address and phone number is all that is needed to get the price process in action. Also information on any animals in the yard is helpful. We are not afraid of dogs but just like to be warned of their presence.

Where do we work?

Grand Junction, Fruita, Palisade Area in Colorado.

Since we are a small, reliable operation we have to stay local.
